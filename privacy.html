Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

1. Data We Collect & Process

To provide contract execution services, we collect and process:

  • Identity Information: Full names, email addresses, and phone numbers.
  • Verification Data: National Identification Numbers (NIN) for identity validation, processed securely in accordance with the Nigeria Data Protection Act (NDPA).
  • Signature Data: Vector canvas signature snapshots, OTP verification logs, and audit trail metadata.

2. How We Share Data (Third Parties)

We do not sell your personal data. We securely transmit data to authorized third parties solely to complete your transactions:

  • Identity Verification: Government-approved verification partners to validate NIN details.
  • Payment Processing: Paystack, for processing standard and enterprise subscription payments safely.
  • Infrastructure: Firebase Cloud Services for secure database storage and hosting.

3. Data Security & Retention

All authentication session states and user metadata configurations are stored natively on secure Firebase cloud infrastructure. Signed documents and audit trails are retained securely until a deletion request is initiated by the contract creator, subject to statutory legal record-keeping requirements under Nigerian law.

4. Your Data Protection Rights

Under the Nigeria Data Protection Act (NDPA), you have the right to access, rectify, or request the erasure of your personal data. Recipient data collected solely for contract signing (such as phone numbers for OTP verification) is retained strictly to maintain the integrity of the agreement's immutable audit trail.

5. Document Privacy & Access

The contents of your generated PDFs are private to the signing parties. SignAm systems access document text strictly through automated pipelines—such as using secure AI models via Groq API to parse clauses during the creation wizard—solely to assist in document formatting or enterprise template generation.
