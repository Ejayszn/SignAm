The Problem with Doing Business by Mouth

In Nigeria, trust is expensive. Too many freelancers get ghosted after submission, vendors get blindsided by unexpected client cancellations, and casual loans turn lifelong friends into bitter strangers.

It's not always because people mean bad — it's because memory fades and unwritten terms leave room for convenient excuses. But renting a traditional lawyer for a basic ₦50,000 deal doesn't make financial sense either. We realized everyday Nigerians needed a simple, legal shield that works instantly on a mobile phone.

What We Actually Built

SignAm lets you type your deal terms, verify both parties with NIN and phone OTP, sign with your finger, and share a locked agreement link in under a minute — all from your browser, no app download needed.

Once both parties sign, the contract is permanently locked in our system with full audit trail metadata: timestamps, IP addresses, device info, and verified identity records. It can't be edited, deleted, or denied. That signed PDF becomes court-ready evidence under the Nigerian Evidence Act 2011.
